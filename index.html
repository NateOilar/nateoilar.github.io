Welcome to my portfolio

Hello everyone. I'm an energetic and inquisitive Boise State University student in pursuit of a bachelor's degree in GIMM (Games, Interactive Media, & Mobile Technology). This website enjoyably showcases most of my work from college, and some work from my own free time. Through these experiences, I've begun to build a strong foundation in interactive design, development, and creative problem-solving. As a current second-year student, I am always exploring new tools and technologies to expand my skill set and to better understand the evolving landscape that is digital media.

Short Biography

Prior to college, I was an active duty United States Air Force member. I had served 6 years, with an honorable discharge classification, as an F-15E aircract mechanic moving all over the United States. During this time, I developed a strong sense of discipline, attention to detail, and teamwork skills that I now use in my academic and creative work. Transitioning from the military into higher education has allowed me to pursue my passion for technology, design, and creativity while still continuing to challenge myself in new and meaningful ways. I am particularly interested in scene design and world development, where I can craft immersive environments that tell a story without relying solely on dialogue. I'm drawn to the artistic side of game development and how interactive experiences can be used to convey real-life emotions, perspectives, and personal experiences in a meaningful way.
