Liminal Forest

This is a 3D liminal scary forest environment that I created in unreal engine 5 over the course of 14 weeks. This far and remote place is sat atop a very large mountain with the only way in or out being the pich black tunnels leading into the side of the moutain. The goal is to wander and search through the thick fog for the missing three capitan america plushies hidden at key POIs. There are abandoned cabins, a rusted and creeky playground, watchtowers, and a half submerged jeep in the nearby lake that you should make sure to look for. All of this built and seemingly recently accupied by people, but for some reason... no one is to be seen, at all... Happy hunting! This was my first ever time creating anything in UE5 ever and it was really fun to experimnet and tinker around with procedurally generated content, material creation, skybox lighting and fog, and 3D modeling from scratch within UE5. With the grass and trees within the environment, are (PG) content that we learned to implement in class. There were issues at first with placing the assets and it generating to many of them and crashing the engine. making smaller areas to be affected helped with this issue and allowed the enironment to run in a more stable fashion. I created custom materials for this project as well and added them to the playground within the environment. With reguards to the playground, that is an asset I created entirly from scratch for this environment. The lighting and fog elements posted their own set of challenges and reserch to understand fully, I feel as tho I have the bacis understood and implimented well in this environment. Finally I added alot of ambiant sounds to really make the environment feel scary and alive. The plushies have attenuation attatched and a sound that lets you know your close. The same technique was used for the pond ambient sounds.
